Event Schedule

Open Mic Performers

Runs Saturday from 10:30 AM to 12:30 PM.

  • Sign up at the Main Information Booth starting at 9:30 AM. Slots are first‑come, first‑served.
  • Each performer gets 5 minutes. Singers, musicians, poets, and family acts welcome.
  • All acts must be family‑friendly.
  • A PA system, mic, and one guitar amp are provided.

Good to Know

  • General admission is free. Only two events need tickets: Heritage Concert and Taste of the Springs.
  • Parking: High School lot and Community Center. ADA parking behind City Hall with shuttle service.
  • Pets are not allowed in lawn or food areas. Service animals are welcome.
  • Schedule may change. See the Questions page for more info.
